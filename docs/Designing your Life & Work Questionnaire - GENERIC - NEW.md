**DESIGNING YOUR LIFE  & WORK (THE HEART OF YOU)**

**DEFINITIONS**:  
**Design** (“nature”): you innate traits, talents, personality type, learning styles, etc.  
**Experience** (“nurture”): your wisdom, perspective, skills, assets acquired throughout life that you leverage for your life purpose  
**Destiny**: Future I am innately designed & prepared for by my experiences  
**Passion**: Internal energy I have to pursue something I care deeply about  
**Calling**: External Commission I accept to serve greater good (my choice to serve without regard to personal reward)  
**Fulfillment**: Lasting sense of joy & satisfaction from living out life purpose  
**Life Purpose**: Channeling your Passion (energy) through your Experience and your Design in the service of a greater Calling→ lasting Fulfillment & Meaning.  
**Fulfillment**: varies (joy, success, peace of mind, pleasure, recognition, etc.) \- often things we have suffered for (i.e. passion), sacrificed for). Moving through and using (not avoiding) pain→lasting fulfillment instead of temp pleasure)
